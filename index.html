ATTITUDE FOR GRATITUDE

"Attitude for Gratitude" refers to cultivating a mindset and behaviour that appreciates and acknowledges the positive contributions and actions of our team members. We want to emphasise the importance of acknowledging and celebrating the efforts and impact individuals make, not only in their professional performance but also in their interactions and support towards others.

Nominee Guidelines

When nominating an employee for their contributions in the workplace, it is essential to evaluate whether they have exemplified one of our company's core values or demonstrated a crucial competency relevant to their role. It is also important to acknowledge that sometimes it is not solely about the tasks we perform, but rather the profound impact we have on others. Therefore, we encourage nominations for employees who have exhibited kindness, generosity, or helpfulness, reflecting on the depth of their character and their ability to positively influence those around them.

Submissions & Deadlines

Submissions for the Gratifier of the Month will commence the day following the announcement at our monthly in-office meetings. Please send your submissions via email to nhu.richards@outsell.com and natalie.passus-yang@outsell.com
