ATTITUDE OF GRATITUDE

"Attitude of Gratitude" refers to cultivating a mindset and behaviour that appreciates and acknowledges the positive contributions and actions of our team members. We want to emphasise the importance of acknowledging and celebrating the efforts and impact individuals make, not only in their professional performance but also in their interactions and support towards others.

Nominee Guidelines

When nominating an employee for their contributions in the workplace, consider whether they exemplified one of Outsell's core values or demonstrated competency relevant to their role. It is also important to acknowledge that sometimes it is not solely about the tasks we perform, but rather the profound impact we have on others. We encourage nominations for employees who have exhibited kindness, generosity, or helpfulness, reflecting on the depth of their character and their ability to influence those around them.

Submissions & Deadlines

Submissions for the Gratifier of the Month will start the day after our monthly in-office meeting, and will be announced as 'open' via Slack. Please send your submissions via email to nhu.richards@outsell.com and natalie.passus-yang@outsell.com

Deadlines for submissions will be set one week prior to the announcement of Gratifier of the Month, and will be announced as 'closed' via Slack by 4PM CST/CDT.

Announcement

Gratifier of the Month will be announced at our monthly in-office meeting and shared in the #attitudeofgratitude Slack channel for those unable to attend. As a token of appreciation, they will receive a $25 gift card from a list of available options.
